I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Kansas, located in Lawrence, KS. My academic journey began with a B.Sc. from Sharif University of Technology and includes an M.Sc. from KU. My graduate studies have been proudly supported by the Wyatt Memorial Scholarships and the National Science Foundation.

My research mission is to build safer, smarter, and faster control systems for complex, high-dimensional systems represented by machine learning models. I specialize in the intersection of three key fields: optimal control, estimation theory, and machine learning.
