About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Kansas advised by Prof. Huazhen Fang. My research studies optimal control and Bayesian inference for machine-learned dynamical systems (e.g., Neural ODEs) with an emphasis on real-time deployment via GPU acceleration. Interests include inference-in-the-loop control, heavy-tailed motion planning, and physics-aware system identification in robotics and energy systems.
