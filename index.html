About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Kansas advised by Prof. Huazhen Fang. My research studies optimal control and Bayesian inference for machine‑learned dynamical systems (e.g., Neural ODEs) with an emphasis on real‑time deployment via GPU acceleration. I am interested in inference‑in‑the‑loop control, heavy‑tailed motion planning, and physics‑aware system identification, with applications in robotics and energy systems.
