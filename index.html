About

I am a graduate researcher at the University of Kansas working on optimal control and Bayesian inference for machine-learned dynamical systems, with an emphasis on GPU-accelerated, real-time decision making. I build inference-in-the-loop controllers, heavy-tailed motion planners, and physics-aware neural ODE models—validated in robotics and energy systems.
